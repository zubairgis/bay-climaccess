Methods, ethics and reproducibility

How to interpret this portal

01

Decision units

The operational database is organized by settlement, health-facility catchment and climate event/month. Building footprints are summarized and are never treated automatically as households.

02

Source separation

Government, WorldPop/modelled, survey, sensor-observed and preferred operational values remain separate, versioned and auditable.

03

Evidence grades

E0 source, E1 derived, E2 internally checked, E3 validated/published, E4 survey corrected and E5 action/outcome linked.

04

Privacy and clinical governance

Public files contain approved aggregates only. Contacts, individual health records, calls and referrals require a secure role-based system. The portal does not diagnose or prescribe.
