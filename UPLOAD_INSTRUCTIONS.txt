IMPORTANT EVIDENCE NOTES
- Settlement populations are WorldPop 2025 ward totals allocated to GRID3 settlement polygons.
- They are not household enumeration results.
- Candidate catchments are derived from wards and Secondary facilities within each LGA.
- They are not official health-service catchments.
- Government, survey, service, validation and outcome values remain empty where evidence was not supplied.
